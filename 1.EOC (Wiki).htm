IV. Arc Summaries

  • Arc 1 – Beginnings (Ch 1–25): Xiao Lin grows, father kidnapped
  • Arc 2 – Wilderness & Growth (Ch 26–45): Awakens talent, meets rivals
  • Arc 3 – Sovereign Realm (Ch 46–80): Arena battles, reputation grows
  • Arc 4 – Top 10 & Ancestral Awakening (Ch 81–110): Top 10 duels, Azure Dragon inheritance, hints of god ascention
  • Arc 5 – Ascension to Godhood (Ch 111–152): Battle with Tianmo, father rescued, Xiao Lin and Lin Er ascend, golden age begins
